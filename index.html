About ISCMGEH South Asia 2025

We are excited to launch the South-Asian edition of the 4th International Student Conference on Medical Geology and Environmental Health (ISCMGEH), a virtual event focused on empowering the next generation of scientists in understanding the intersection of Earth's processes and human health.

üèÜ Medical Geology: An Emerging Field

The connection between medicine and geology is more profound than it may appear. Medical geology is an emerging interdisciplinary field that explores how geological materials and processes affect human and ecosystem health.
